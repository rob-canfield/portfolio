Psychic Game

Test your psychic powers against this simple JavaScript game. Playing is easy: use the keyboard to guess what letting the computer is thinking of. You have nine guesses before the computer wins.

Created July 2019 under instruction of Matt Banz at the University of Utah.

Ocarina Of Time Trivia

10 questions to test your knowlege of this classic N64 game. You'll have 10 seconds to answer each question. Ready, set, go!

Created August 2019 under instruction of Matt Banz at the University of Utah.

Giphy Search

This app uses the Giphy API to allow users to search GIFs of their choice. Stop and start the GIFs by clicking on them.

Created August 2019 under instruction of Matt Banz at the University of Utah.

Crystal Collector

You've been assignes a mighty task, Wizard. Match your total score with the random number assigned at the beginning of the game by clicking the four crystals. Each crystal is assigned a random value at the beginning of the game. No two crystals are equal to each other.

The game is designed to alert you when you have won or lost. The game will then reset the random number value and all of the crystal values.

Created August 2019 under instruction of Matt Banz at the University of Utah.
